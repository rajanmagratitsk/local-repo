Scooter & Motorcycle Maintenance Guide

Learn easy tips to keep your bike healthy

1. Engine Oil Check

  • Change engine oil every 2000 - 3000 km.
  • Use recommended oil grade.
  • Low oil can damage engine.
Tip: Check oil level every week.

2. Tyre Care

  • Maintain correct tyre pressure.
  • Check tyre cracks and wear.
  • Replace old tyres on time.
Tip: Low air reduces mileage.

3. Brake Maintenance

  • Check front and rear brakes regularly.
  • Replace brake pads if worn out.
  • Adjust brake lever properly.

4. Battery Care

  • Start vehicle regularly.
  • Keep terminals clean.
  • Check horn and lights.

5. Chain Care (Motorcycle)

  • Clean chain every 500 km.
  • Lubricate with chain lube.
  • Adjust chain tension.

6. Washing Tips

  • Wash with clean water.
  • Do not spray high pressure on meter and wiring.
  • Dry after washing.

7. Riding Habits

  • Avoid sudden acceleration.
  • Do not overload scooter.
  • Warm engine before long ride.
